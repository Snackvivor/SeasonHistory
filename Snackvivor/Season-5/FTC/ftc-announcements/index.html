Belle: First of all, I just wanna say thank you for taking the time to read this and that I'm very much looking forward to answering questions about my game. I know it wasn't perfect, but I'm proud of it all the same and I worked hard to get to FTC. I started this game with a strong alliance in the Ravioli Renegades with Kevin and Maple, and that relationship with Maple would continue all through the game to the end here - but it wasn't the only relationship that helped me in this game. My OG tribe started rough, with us attending three tribal councils in a row and with my name thrown out by Matt at the third tribal council. But the relationships I built on the tribe made the vote a unanimous vote on Matt, with only him voting for me. On my first tribe, I voted right every single time. It wasn't until my third tribe swap that I was unaware of which direction the vote would be going - and this was the only time I didn't know where it was going as well. I would vote in the minority on the Quack vote to secure a relationship with Benno and I voted for Pie knowing that Pie would be using his Steal a Vote on Hayes to flip the vote onto Maple. Throughout the game, I knew I was playing from the bottom, and I made sure others knew it too. I painted myself as an easy extra vote to be picked up, happy to vote where people told me, as long as they told me information. This led to me knowing where the vote was going at every turn, and where advantages where. Hayes told me about Quack's idol, I knew that Hayes and Maple had a swop, as well as the second swop that Cetus gave Hayes. I knew about Pie's steal a vote. I also knew that by painting myself as an extra number that could be picked up, people would be unlikely to vote me out and choose to go for bigger targets instead. And the one time my name did come up on parchment, I won the immediate next immunity challenge to ensure my safety. For a large portion of the game I was vulnerable to being voted out, and at every turn, I stayed. So thank you for reading, and I can't wait to talk to all of you again.

Cetus: My premerge was strong, forming very solid allies in Benno, Quack, and Eck who all helped me get through the tribal phase of the game completely unscathed. Merge came around, however, and my position was completely toppled on its base with Eck leaving and the entirety of F8. However, my social game enabled me to get key information and establish solid relationships with people like Pie or Maple who I would use to try and fracture the four person alliance at the center of the merge. I dropped info about Quack's idol to Maple which gave me an inroad with him and Hayes. Once Nicko was gone, however, I took to bolstering my relationship with Pie who was both a sword to aim at Hayple and a shield who would go first instead. All in all, my social game was amazing and allowed me to maneuver tough positions by building connections with people in the merge despite having only met half of the cast by then. I also managed my threat level well and constantly inflated the threat level of other players like Pie. I was adaptable in this incredibly turbulent merge by assessing the threats in the game (like Hayes and Maple being in the four alliance) and navigating that by making myself valuable to them such that they would set their aims on the other duo, Pie and Nicko. And last by not least... Was my persistence and resilience in the face of seemingly insurmountable odds. No matter what shit was thrown at me, I stayed strong and despite merge throwing heavy emotional curveballs like F8, tie votes, Eck's blindside, Maple idoling himself, I pushed myself harder to find new avenues to survive and I managed to claw my way through all the way until the end because I wanted that victory above all else. I truly believe I put the most into this ORG out of anyone and I'm right here just one vote away from the win I so desperately want. So, to the jury, please don't let it all have been for nothing.
