Today we will be talking about Rodger Bingham from season 2, The Australian Outback. Father-daughter dynamics crop up all the time in Survivor, such examples include Neleh and Pascal in Marquesas and Carolyn/Carson to name a few. My favourite depiction of this trope is the original, that being the relation between Rodger and his close relationship with Elisabeth Filarski. As this is still the early days of strategy these two players form a solid bond that carries them into the final five of the game, and even though I knew the finale came down to Colby Donaldson and Tina, I was incredibly relieved to see Rodger make it as far as he did. Despite everything that happened over the course of the season such as skupin’s incredibly scary injury and subsequent medvac as well as the whole surviving in the hot desert essentially Rodger stands as a beacon of hope of friendliness amongst this sometimes-bitter cast, strongly contrasting the likes of Jerri and Keith. Many of this “old sweet character” have come and past in years since but none hit as hard as Rodger did for me, I genuinely could not help but smile every time he came on screen. #55, exactly 200
