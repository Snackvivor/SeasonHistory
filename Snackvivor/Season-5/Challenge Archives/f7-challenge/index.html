7:33 PM
Welcome to Cheese’s Fine Dining you 7 lucky individuals! You’ve all been hired just now! Yippee! Hope you have a good time at your first shift and nothing bad happens. It won’t be that bad... I think. Here’s how this challenge is gonna go down. For every command you type in, you will be given a hint, ranging from a puzzle to a light read. Each of these hints are all pointing to a bigger theme that links all of them together (think of this sort of like Connections: You’re connecting the answers to each of these hints to a theme). Your task will be to figure what the heck that even is, with the person getting it with the least amount of hints and guesses getting immunity. Now let’s get logistical.
7:34 PM
Typing in !f7-hint1 in your submissions channel will start the challenge and give you the first hint. I will emphasize that completing these are not required and screenshots of each completed puzzle are not necessary, but you may be missing out on crucial information if you don’t finish them. When you’re done with a hint and if you think you got the answer, type in ! and then your answer (!example). You’ll get a message to ping prod if you’re right and nothing if you didn’t quite get it. If you get nothing from the first hint, typing in !f7-hint2 and so on and so forth up to 10 will give you subsequent hints. If you can’t get it after 10, feel free to ping prod for the answer, but just know that you won’t be in the running for immunity if you do. The breakdown for scoring will be as follows: First, whoever gets the answer in the least amount of hints will win immunity. If there’s a tie there, then it goes to the person with the least guesses in total throughout all of the hints. Finally, if both of those are tied, it’ll come down to time. Also no talking about this challenge until after results! Good luck on your shift folks!
7:38 PM
Note: You have unlimited guesses per hint, no limit there at all!
