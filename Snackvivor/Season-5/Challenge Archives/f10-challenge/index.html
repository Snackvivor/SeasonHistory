Let’s get into explaining the actual challenge, using my profile as an example.
7:12 PM
PHASE ONE - SWIPING RIGHT:
- You will have four categories on your Compatibility Meter. Attitude, Humor, Social and Hardness. For each category, your choice of trait matters. - Our Pastasonas will also have a dating profile, exactly like your own! Except you cannot see their compatibility meter, nor their traits. It will look like this:
7:13 PM
7:13 PM
- You will have to guess which traits the Pastasonas have and try to match them! For every trait that you have in common with the Pastasona you choose to date, you will receive TWO LOVE POINTS. For each trait that you are one off from you will receive ONE LOVE POINT. If you are two off of the trait you will receive ZERO LOVE POINTS. - After you submit which Mascot you will be dating, your love point total will be calculated and your date’s full character card will be revealed to you!
7:13 PM
7:13 PM
- For example, let’s assume that Ani’s humour trait is Sarcastic. If I also put sarcastic, I’d get two points. If I put Punny, I’d get one point. If I put Deadpan, I’d get no points. - This applies for all four categories. The maximum number of points you can receive is EIGHT. - There will be hints in the Pastasona’s description, use them wisely!
7:14 PM
PHASE TWO - DATE NIGHT:
- Next, you gotta take your lovely Pastasona on a date! - There will be ten locations for you to choose from, each with their own set of preferred traits. You must guess, based on your Pastasona’s traits and the hints provided on the location card, which place your Pastasona would like to go the best! - Each Mascot also has an OPTIMAL LOCATION. If you take your Mascot to their perfect date place, you will get an extra two love points! - Very similar to our first phase! But this time, the full location card is not revealed to you - So you won’t know how many points you received this round. - But that’s not all, because each date has its own activity that you must complete there.
7:14 PM
PHASE THREE - STRESS TO IMPRESS:
- The activity that you’ll be completing on the date will also be hinted at in the location description, so you’ll have to prepare yourself for what’s ahead! - Your Mascot will have a set time that they will have completed the location’s activity in - If you get 30 seconds before/after the Mascot’s time you get four love points, if you get less than 30 seconds before/after the Mascot’s time you get eight love points, if you get more than 30 seconds before/after the Mascot’s time you get two love points. - For example, let’s say that the location I take Ani to has a Kenken mini-game!
7:14 PM
7:15 PM
- After inserting a command, I will complete the activity and submit my time! - For example, I completed this Kenken in 6:37. It is then revealed that Ani’s time was 6:15. That’s within 30 seconds of Ani’s time, so I get four points! - You will not be told your partner’s time. You will complete your mini game and the date will be over. Exact times, comparisons and final point totals will be revealed during results.
7:15 PM
7:15 PM
I got 12 love points for my date with Ani!
7:15 PM
The player who received the most love points on their date wins!
7:15 PM
You will also be asked to guess the total amount of love points every player will receive across the board. This will be our tiebreaker.
7:15 PM
TLDR:
- You will make a dating profile, choosing one of three traits for four separate categories. After you @ production to approve your profile, it will be locked in and you may not change any traits. - You will attempt to match yourself and your traits to a Pastasona. The closer your traits are to theirs, the more points you get. - You will attempt to match your date to a location. The closer THEIR traits are to the location’s, the more points you get. - You will attempt to clear that location’s mini game. The less time you take to complete said mini game in comparison to your date’s time, the more points you get. - The player with the most love points, and by definition the date that was the most successful, wins the first merge individual immunity of Snackvivor: Pastadise Island!
