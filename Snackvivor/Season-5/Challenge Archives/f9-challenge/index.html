# F9 - Pastaround The World
7:29 PM
For this challenge you will all be given 10 images of locations that could be anywhere in the world!
7:29 PM
Your job this challenge is to figure out the exact google maps coordinate location for each image and for bonus points at the end identify the theme of the images!
7:29 PM
Here is an example image with some details on how to find some of the information we are asking for:
7:29 PM
7:29 PM
As you can see with this image we are near the Sydney Opera House in Sydney, Australia!
7:29 PM
However, as you can see this image is across the water somewhere from the Opera House, and THAT is what we are looking for!
7:29 PM
Once you think you have found the location in Google Maps street view, you can use Google Maps to get the exact coordinates by copying the coordinates from the URL bar!
7:30 PM
Here is an example of where you can find the coordinates once you find the location:
7:30 PM
7:30 PM
An example submission for one image may look like:
7:30 PM

7:30 PM
Here is how the scoring system works:
7:30 PM
2 points per correct coordinate location (With 1 point being awarded if you are close - Within a google street view hop or two) (If you don't know what that means feel free to ask!)
7:30 PM
2 points awarded if you guess the theme (With 1 point being awarded if you have the right idea but are a little off!)
7:30 PM
That means there are 22 possible points you can earn!
1
7:30 PM
Tie-breaker will go to the player who submits the fastest (you must submit your theme guess with your image guesses), so when you are done please type DONE with your final submission and DO NOT EDIT anything!
7:30 PM
If you need to make an edit please make a new list rather than editing!
7:30 PM
We highly recommend doing this on a PC and not on mobile - While it is possible to get the coordinates on mobile, it is a bit more tedious to do so!
7:30 PM
(Inquire for further instructions if you plan to do it on mobile)
7:31 PM
When you are ready to start run !f9-start!
7:31 PM
NOTE: Be EXTRA sure to check your coordinates and make sure you don't accidentally use the same ones twice / assign the values to the wrong location! Also make sure you do all 10!
7:31 PM
That's pretty much it! If you have any questions please let us know and good luck!
