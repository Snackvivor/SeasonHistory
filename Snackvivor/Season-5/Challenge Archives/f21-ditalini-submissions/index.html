20 Hello my name is Pyers and this is my review of Snackaversary Choose Your Own Adventure, a youtube based choose your own adventure game that commences here:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-pioiUIWCcM and concludes here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4Ec4_OKfZss. I think I pretty much speedran this choose your own adventure as I always made sure to choose the option that I felt would get me to Pastadise island the fastest. I commend the game designers for offering a more efficient route for pro gamers such as myself. Despite spending not very much time to "stop and smell the roses" if you will, I still enjoyed the cute character design and food-based mascots presented throughout the game, such as Rocket (who I assume is a popsicle person?), Apple, and Pecan. I especially appreciated the inclusion of "Piesylvania" as I am personally an ambassador for a Philadelphia based pastry community organization centered around pies. Inclusion of my specific subcommunity, however unintentional, truly warmed "the conkles of my heart" if you will and made me feel at home. After departing from Piesylvania I appreciated that I had the opportunity to reassure my new good magical friend Apple that it would totally work to "poof" me to the penultimate destination the cheese pier. Stowing away on a boat to reach Pastadise island was an exciting final twist, and I appreciate that I was rewarded for remaining resolute in my desire for pasta and not breaking my fast for whatever "food" they had lying around a boat and getting called out by someone annoying. Thank you and go pies.
