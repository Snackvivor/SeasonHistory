Welcome @Margarita to Tribal Council! As you are all aware this tribal is a little bit different than the norm as a pair is being eliminated! How this works is you will all still get an individual vote and you will vote for another individual player (not yourself!) The pair that receives the most amount of votes will be eliminated, with the player getting the higher amount of votes in the pair getting 14th and the player with the lesser amount of votes in the pair getting 13th. If there is a tie, we will determine placement based on tasks completed in the challenge. If two (or more) pairs tie for most votes, everyone in that pair will be ineligible to vote, regardless of how many votes the individual got in each respective pair. If the revote still ties, it will go to rocks and the pairs with the tied votes will become immune, and all other pairs (sans immune pairs) will be eligible for rocks. You will have until the end of tribal tomorrow (Which will start roughly around 6:40 PM EST and end roughly around 7:00 PM EST) to vote! Once the deadline has occured, your vote cannot be changed! When live tribal starts (Which is around 6:40 PM EST tomorrow) the tribal host will ask conversations about your tribe, your tribal, and the game in general. You are not required to be online for live Tribal Council, but it's more fun if you are! When the deadline hits, the live advantage period will begin. You will have 5 minutes to play any relevant advantages (Please read #game-rules for more details regarding advantages). If you wish to play an advantage but will not be online at this time, you may pre-play it in your submissions channel and we will play it for you. To submit your vote, use any image-editing software to write or type the player's name on our season parchment (obtained by running !parchment), then send it in your submissions channel. You may decorate your parchment, but make sure it is clear who you are voting for. If you have any questions, don't hesitate to ask. You may not vote for Prince and Seared. (edited)
