The Longest, Shortest Road I've Ever Seen

Damn. What a pain in the ass challenge for a mini What's up Hungry Specs? My name's Seared, resident starving artist and all-around chill dude. I was dragged here by my partner, Prince, but I honestly don't mind. Reminded me that I love a good social-based ORG I'm a 19 year-old screenwriter looking to make his way into the TV industry, and, while none of those skills carry over well to ORGs, I usually pride myself on my social game and ability to connect with people on a personal level. Whether that'll work with this group remains to be seen This first day has honestly been a whirlwind ️. Maybe I should have expected it. I mean, by nature, a mini's gotta be fast-paced. Still, the hosts came in SWINGING with such an intense challenge, stacked on top of an effective auto-merge. Finding my footing will be half the battle. Right now, the immediate stand-out for me is:
Kells - I mean, goddamn. What a terrifying impression. Dude's funny af, super enjoyable to talk to, but also really intense. Don't get me wrong, I love it; but I also feel like he's the type to play pretty hard, despite what he says. I mean, he was trying to rally me to an "anti- VC meta" vote when I've hardly talked to half the cast in the first place! That's crazy.... Annnnnd the exact type of crazy I'd rather have on my side than against me. He could be a wild card, and I'd rather keep him close than far.
Outside of Kells though, I don't feel like I've talked to anyone enough to really feel tight with them. That's sorta terrifying, all things considered, but it just means I'll have to work double-time on my social game tomorrow. All in all, I'm excited to see where this season goes. I don't have high hopes for winning this challenge, but if Prince and I crush it, that would be the dream (edited)
