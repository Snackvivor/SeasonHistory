Aurora 6/16/2024 5:00 PM
Contrary to the other two, I don't think that there was any single realization I had that made the biggest impact on me getting here, but rather I'd say it was the final 6. The greedy, more tempting move for me to make in that moment would have been to vote out Gengar and aim for an FTC with Toast and Caro, who were viewed at more negatively. However, I held true to what I knew I had to do, and cut Toast, guaranteeing myself a spot right here and setting myself up in the exact position I wanted to be in. This is something that has eaten away at me since super early in the game. By far my biggest mistake in the game was not making a move in the safari until the swap. Not only did this completely stunt any chance I had at finding advantages before anyone else, but also put me in a position where I was playing from behind socially while most others had already talked to people cross tribe within the Safari. It set me back big time and I would absolutely have done more with Safari if I could go back. Winning would mean everything to me. This has, without exaggeration, been the most meaningful ORG I've ever played because for the first time ever it felt like I was really the one playing it. I've won the game before, yes, but I wasn't proud of my wins. They felt so empty, and I felt like I didn't have to do anything and lucked into those spots. This season, it didn't have to be another season of Crossbow having one thing go wrong and giving up. This season, Aurora had multiple things go wrong, but she kept fighting and made it all the way to FTC even. This is my first ORG as Aurora, and I'd love more than almost anything if it could be my first win as Aurora too.
