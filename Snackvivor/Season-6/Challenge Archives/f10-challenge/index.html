# F10: Chopping Questions
9:00 PM
This challenge will have 3 parts so be aware of how it's gonna work.
9:01 PM

Part 1: Individual Questions

You will answer a series of questions about your tribemates. Please answer each question honestly. Each answer must be one of the other nine remaining members of your tribe (you cannot answer yourself for this part.) Your answers for this part will not be publicly revealed. Discussion of the answers is NOT allowed. You will have until 9:00 a.m. EST tomorrow to submit your answers, but the earlier, the better. Questions without a majority answer will be thrown out when the challenge plays out.
9:01 PM

Part 2: Guessing

You will now answer the questions again, but now your goal is to now guess which answer was the most common for each question. The answer could potentially be your name. Each castaway with a correct prediction will be allowed to cut a section of rope holding up a cookie with the castaway’s name they want eliminated. Three cuts of the rope would send the cookie into the glass of milk, where it will crumble, thus eliminating that player from the challenge. The last person standing wins immunity!!
9:02 PM

Part 3: Chopping

Each tribe member will then need to create a chop list. You will rank your fellow tribemates from #1 to #9 in the order you want to see them eliminated. This is your priority order for who you want to chop in the challenge if you get a correct answer. I will skip over a name if the person has already been eliminated from the challenge. Do not include yourself in the chop list.
9:02 PM
To get access to the questions, just put the command !f10-chopping in your submissions channel. Answering the questions is not timed in any way so feel free to put the command in to see the questions and take your time with it (edited)
