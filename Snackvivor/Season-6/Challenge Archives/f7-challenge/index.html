A WEEK ON THE FARM One of my favorite games of all time is Stardew Valley so I thought omg lets make a challenge based on it and here is what I came up with. The goal of this challenge will be to end up with the most money as possible and there will be a variety of ways to achieve this. But first the ground rules. This game will take place over 7 “days” and each day you can perform 2 actions, alongside a final festival (These days and actions can be performed at any time; it is just flavor text). Well what can you do these days? I am glad you asked!!
8:20 PM
PLANTING CROPS I will provide you a list of crops all with different prices and values and special effects. You can spend one action to buy as many as you want then automatically plant them, provided you have the funds of course. These crops will take x amount of days to grow. An Example would be let's say you buy a crop that takes 2 days to grow. You buy it on day 1 then it will be growing day 1 and 2 and finally will be grown day 3 where you will get your money (this does not take an action you automatically get the money. (edited)
8:21 PM
8:21 PM
FISHING This will be a game of skill every day will have a different fishing mini game that you must pass in order to get 100 dollars.. You will have to complete the task given in the time frame also given (you will have to time yourself, but I will be around to do official timings to see if you pass) If you pass great you get money!! If you don’t pass you don’t get money :( . You can only fish once per day and the command is !f7-fishing-day-x (with x being whatever day you are on)
8:22 PM
MINING This one is simple, it is just an rng chance to get money with the odds being 30 dollars - 15/25 60 dollars - 7/25 120 dollars - 3/25 Plain and simple. The command for this is !f7-mining
8:23 PM
GIFT GIVING Unlike the other two this does not directly give you money but instead gives you a bonus. You must give a gift twice in order for it to happen. Here is the list of possible rewards for gifts. You can only use each individual person once. Penny - Makes crops worth 5 dollars more each (Max 10 day) Abigail - Gives you an extra 30 seconds per fishing game and makes fish worth 150 dollars Sam - Makes all current crops grow on the next day Shane - Gives you 5 minutes for the final festival Wizard - Buffs mining to be (new command is !f7-mining-wizard) 10/25 - 40 dollars 10/25 - 80 dollars 5/25 - 140 dollars (edited)
8:23 PM
ANIMAL CARE Just gives you 40 dollars, that is all.
8:23 PM
FINAL FESTIVAL Finally we have the festival, this is something everyone will participate in whenever you want. You will have 30 minutes to get the best score in a mystery game. The order in which you all score will give you money based on your placement. So first place among you will get the most money and so on. Here is the amounts 1st 250 2nd 175 3rd 125 4th 75 5th 50 6th 25 7th 0
8:23 PM
The command for this event will be !f7-festival and as soon as you are given the link your time will start.
