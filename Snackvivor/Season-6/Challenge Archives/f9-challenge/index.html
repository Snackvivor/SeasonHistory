8:36 PM

8:38 PM

8:39 PM

Make a Sale: Round 1: Sell as many boxes as you like/have in your Stock, making $1/box Round 2: Sell as many boxes as you like/have in your Stock, making $3/box Round 3: Sell as many boxes as you like/have in your Stock, making $5/box Everything Must Go! - After Round 3 is resolved, each box of cookies remaining in a Scout’s stock is sold, making $3/box. Actions taken the previous round that would normally affect a next round take place as usual. Positive Actions: (by the way, if an action has a (2) next to it, and you want to do it, that's the only action you get that turn) (Q) Flash Sale (1) - Lose $5, target earns x2 profit for this round’s MaS. Marketing Rally (1) - Lose $5, target earns x3 profit for the next round’s MaS. Bake, bake, bake! (1) - Lose $5, target gains 15 boxes of cookies. Hire Security (2) - Lose $10, if anyone tries to perform a negative action during this round on you, they lose half of their stock, rounded down. RoI (3) - Lose $300 to invest into the Scout Stock Market. HODL for two rounds, and gain an immunity idol. Negative Actions: (Q) Shop Sabotage (1) - Reduces the value of each of the target’s boxes by 25% for this round. Poison the Flour (1) - Reduces the value of each of the target’s boxes by 50% for the next round. Box Burning (1) - Reduce target stock by half for the next round. Armed Robbery (1/2) - Steal half of target’s money. If you use 2 actions to perform Armed Robbery, and another person has also tried to do this action on the same target, you automatically go before them. Hire a Hitman (3) - Lose $1000, Kill a Little Girl. (edited)
