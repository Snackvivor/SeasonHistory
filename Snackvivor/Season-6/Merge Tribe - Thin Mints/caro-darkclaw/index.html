I'd be happy to share more about that! I kinda grew up never relating much to people, but I always really related to cartoon characters and animals, I think because their expressions are more animated, and easy to read! Rabbits are energetic (I have ADHD and am often hyperactive), rabbits are smart / wise, and I see myself as someone who has been through a great deal of adversity, and the type of rabbit I based my sona off of is also native to where I've lived my whole life! Rabbits are also docile and kind, which I hope to be in my day to day life. So my sona represents both my ideals, and the parts of myself I'm most proud of. After choosing the species, I got some art of it, and with time I felt more connected to the character, and it helped me start to express myself in a way where I could feel proud of who I was, and over time the character just became a part of me. It's the me I want to be, and it inspires me to be the best version of myself every day.
9:54 AM
