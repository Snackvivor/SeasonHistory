# F10 - Breakfast Math
8:18 PM
This challenge will run in two parts but is really easy...
8:19 PM
Part 1 - Search and Find: You will be given ten images where you have to count what you are asked for. You need to save those numbers for Part 2. To search you need to input these commands in your submission channel. Your time will start since you put the first command and ends when you have typed all 10 numbers. !f10-image1 !f10-image2 !f10-image3 !f10-image4 !f10-image5 !f10-image6 !f10-image7 !f10-image8 !f10-image9 !f10-image10 The numbers you submit can't be edited, if you do a wrong count and want to fix a number, the time will count since you started the command the first time until you fix the number.
8:20 PM
8:20 PM
Part 2 - Math Puzzle: After you find the 10 numbers, you will be given a math puzzle to solve with nine of those ten numbers (yes, one of the numbers will be left over). To access the puzzle you need to use the command !f10-math in your submission channel. This will start your time for the second part. To solve this puzzle you have to use PEMDAS. Once you solve the puzzle, send a screenshot with the solution to stop your time. The winner of this immunity challenge will be who does both parts the fastest!!
