# F8: FLAPJACK FALLS FIBBAGE
8:34 PM
This isn’t exactly Fibbage to a tee, there’s been some minor alterations, so listen up! This will be split up into 3 phases: First Phase! In your submissions channel, you will receive a prompt. No making up a fun fact here, all of these questions will be something you can answer on a personal level. You should answer this question truthfully, as you will get points for every person that correctly identifies your truth (more details later). Second Phase! Next, you will receive 7 prompts that aren’t your own. This is where you are trying to answer the questions in a way that will stump the other castaways and make them think the person in the prompt said your answer. You want a really good lie here. Third Phase! Finally, the 7 prompts will be handed back to you in a pretty picture with your lies and 7 other answers! Your goal is to find the truth for each prompt and avoid those dastardly little fibs. And that’s all there really is to it, nothing too complex. Scoring Also very simple! For each truth you find, you get 3 points. For each person that finds the truth on your prompt, you get 2 points. And for each person you stump with a lie, you get 1 point per person fooled. Tiebreakers will go in reverse point order, so it'll be settled by total number of people fooled, then by total people that found your truth, and finally by each truth you find. Additional Rules and Tidbits You may not indicate in any of your prompts that you are the person writing the message. I will reject any prompt that hints this in any way, don’t be dumb lol.
8:35 PM
If your lie is too close to the submitted truth, you will be asked to change your answer. Sucks, but at least you know you’re going in the right direction! If you submit the exact same lie as another person, I will be submitting a lie in your place based on time of submission. Get creative, I'm not good at this lying thing! Please try to keep your answers brief. While there is a fair bit of room on the images, I don’t want them to be illegible. I’m not going to place a hard limit on this but if your answer is too much I’ll ask for a slightly shorter version. Don’t talk about this challenge until after deadline! Because of the simplicity of this challenge, ideally, the first phase is done later tonight and the other two phases can be completed later in the day tomorrow. Other than that, I hope you enjoy it and let me know if you have any questions!
