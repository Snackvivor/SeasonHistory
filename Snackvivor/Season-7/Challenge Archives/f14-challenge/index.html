Similar to the first time we ran this challenge yall are going to be pairing up into teams of two (with one team of three). Once you get into your teams, each team will have to choose one of the following games to participate in where one member will do one thing and the other will do the other (in the case of the team of 3, 2 people will be together on one side your choice!!). Just what are these games? Glad you asked!!!
trueble cleffieable 8/23/2024 8:20 PM
Game 1 - Codenames In this game one of you will be the clue setter and they other will be the solver. The clue setter will begin by receiving a list of words with 10 of them being the assigned clue words you are trying to get your partner to guess then 1 being an assassin word that will automatically earn you 0 points. Each clue can only be one word long. You will provide all of the clues in advance and we will give the solver your clues one by one. The clue solver will then go. They will get one clue and MUST guess at minimum 2 words. Every word they get correct you get one point. The second you guess a word that is not a clue word your run ends and the points you have gathered to this point will be your final score. And again if you guess the assassin word you get 0 points. Here is an example of a board you may be provided. Red words are the ones you wanna get while orange is the assassin word!
Dinocicle 8/23/2024 8:21 PM
Game 2 - Wavelengths One of you will receive a pair of two words that are on opposite sides of a spectrum alongside a number from 1 to 100. Your goal will be to give a two word clue for each spectrum that your partner will then guess to try and get as close as possible to the given number. For example you may get the spectrum FROZEN - FLUID with the number 80 and you may use the clue MOVING RIVER and the closer your partner gets to 80 the better the points. The scoring is as follows. WITHIN 5 - 3 Points 6 - 10 off - 2 Points 11 - 15 off - 1 Point Anything more than that is NOTHIN. Hosts can reject some clues at will depending on how they are worded
trueble cleffieable 8/23/2024 8:22 PM
and finally... Game 3 - Scrabble And new to the thats my snack library we have scrabble. In this game one player will be given a scrabble board and will create 3 hands of letters (anything goes) One at a time you must create a board with the letters in your hand. Each new hand will build upon the previous board created. For example your first hand might spell out the word FRIENDSHIP then the second board must have that word in same spot. The solver will then be given the board and all three hands at the same time. Their goal will be to try and figure out what words the clue solver were trying to put and where. You will be given one point for every scrabble piece placed in the right position. Here is an example of an acceptable board state!

For this challenge (this is inspired by the Legend of Zelda btw) you will be given a blank excel sheet with a grid of blank rooms and a wide selection of possible rooms you must fill them with. The challenge will start by typing the command !f12-map-makers-start, this will start the challenge and describe the current room you are in and which ways you can go. You can then type one of the commands provided to go through a ‚Äúdoor‚Äù which will then describe the new room you are in. The goal of this challenge is to create the most accurate map with the text you are provided. SOME THINGS TO TAKE NOTE OF. You can only use rooms provided in the list of the blank sheet I will provide shortly, you can not combine rooms into one. There are two different kinds of ‚Äúrooms‚Äù : walkable rooms which are the ones containing doors; these are rooms you can actually reside in. While there are also extra decoration rooms that are things that may be outside of walkable rooms. Please pay attention to everything the text is saying. It all has a purpose. You can only do commands going to a room adjacent to you or commands in the room itself (there will be some extra commands you will find out about in the dungeon) Meaning no teleporting around, if you ever perform an illegal action there will be a penalty at the end. A room will not change from its initial value on the map you make (for example if you do something in a room that activates something the room you use will still be what you walked in first). I also recommend zooming out on the sheet just to be able to see the whole grid at once, along with doing this on a computer. Finally not every room will be used (both room spaces and room options) so do not worry if not everything is used. (edited)
