Welcome to the Hungry Draft!

In this game, you will choose 4 players that you think will go the farthest in the game. Please select one player as your Winner Pick which will count for double points, and three additional players. If you choose the players that have the lowest sum of placements, you will win $10! Note that you must have a unique draft, so if you submit an invalid draft you will be asked to make a new one in a new message. You must stay in the server until the end of the season to earn the prize. You have until the end of the first challenge to pick! Drafts can be changed at any time before the deadline, but please reply to your original message if you want to change Draft messages should have only put the 4 player names you are selecting, and nothing more.
