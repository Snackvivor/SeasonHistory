Your tribe will be coming up with a cheese-themed restaurant that you’ll be designing a Menu for - It’s up to you guys to decide how nuanced (or un-nuanced) your cheese inclusion is within the general concept. It can be as traditional or as digital as you’d like, it can be a canvas of any shape - You can even choose how refined or how silly to make it, as long as you’re fitting the general criteria. You can have a maximum of two pages for your Menu, and a minimum of one. Neither option holds more favor than the other, but we can’t have our judges sifting through twenty pages of Menu for each tribe, unfortunately! You must include a minimum of five dishes. There is not a maximum number, as long as they fit on your canvas. Each dish must include: Title Ingredients Price Every dish must incorporate cheese as an ingredient in some way. You may add illustrations/images/pictures as needed or preferred. Please send us a hard copy of your Menu when it is completed, not a link - a PDF or Image file. Presentation - 15/15 Dishes - 9/15 Restaurant Theming - 8/10 Adherence to Guidelines - 10/10 =42 Coming in strong aesthetically speaking. The implication that the menu remains on fire as it’s in my hands may be questionable health and safety-wise, but adds a unique touch to the immersion. The Soupliette has found a special place in my heart, both for its macabre spirit and the fact that it’s a dish I’d genuinely eat. On the flip side, the Wayward Path sounds like the recipe of an Instagram health and fitness account that believes seed oils cause cancer, the amount of bleu cheese in Gluttony’s Bounty is nearly inedible to the average person, and some of the potential flavor combinations that could be encountered in The Ascent leave me with a few questions.
