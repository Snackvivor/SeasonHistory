Please make the most of your confessionals as it adds to the fun of the game! Spectators love reading these so use them as frequently as you would like. To start you off good on these confessionals, please answer these basic starter questions!
1 - Tell us about yourself and your life! 2 - What are your first reactions about the game that lies ahead? 3 - How do you plan on playing this season to become the sole survivor?
Good luck and have fun!
