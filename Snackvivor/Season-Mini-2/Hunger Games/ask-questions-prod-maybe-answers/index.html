Are there four stats or 13 It's just four right
Fish 11/9/2024 11:27 AM
There are four categories to put stats into. Typical DnD has 6 - Wisdom, Intelligence, Charisma, Constitution, Dexterity, Strength. We removed Charisma and Constitution since Charisma is basically however y'all want to socialize, and for constitution you all get the same amount of HP (Which right now is 10 but there's a non-0 chance we bump that up a bit before the game starts if we feel it's too low). The default for each stat is 3, so on paper you could do 3, 3, 3, 3 and have no perks but also no drawbacks (outside of your district buffs). On the other hand, if you feel confident that you don't need some skills as much as the others, you can distribute your points unevenly and take the buffs and penalties as they come.
