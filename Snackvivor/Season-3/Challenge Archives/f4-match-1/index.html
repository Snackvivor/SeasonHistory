the-dice-master BOT 08/23/2023 5:44 PM
This bot emulates the game Dicey Dungeons but with far less cards, far less rules and is multiplayer only! You can setup decks and play matches where you will go head to head with your opponent using randomly drawn dice to play cards that will affect yourself or your opponent. Each turn you draw four dice which you can use to activate the six cards you have selected prior to starting the game. If you are the first player, on your first turn you will only roll two dice! It does not matter what slot you set the cards to - You need specific dice to activate certain cards but what slot you set the card to is irrelevant! To add yourself to the player roster, type !create-player <@UserRole> - For example: !create-player @Fish To get the list of available cards you can choose from, type !list-cards To select which cards you will use for your battle, type !set-card <cardSlot> <cardName> - For example: !set-card 1 sword-slash Once your cards are all selected you can view your loadout with !loadout To start a game type !start-game <@UserRole1> <@UserRole@>! For example !start-game @Fish @Sappho On your turn you will be randomly given four dice, and you can play your dice on a card you have slotted by typing !play <cardName> <diceNumber> - For example: !play sword-slash 3 You start the game with 30 health. Some cards allow you to regain health but you cannot gain health over 30! Once you are ready to end your turn, type !end-turn If the game is over after a player types !end-turn, the game will end and the winner will be declared! The game has a maximum of 30 turns, if the game is not over before 30 turns the player with the highest health value come turn 30 will win. If they have tied health it will go into overtime where the game will end the next turn where a player's health is lower than anothers.
