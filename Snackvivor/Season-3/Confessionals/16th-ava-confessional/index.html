Ava 07/21/2023 9:36 PM
Thank you for the question Sophia! To get more context on the type of strategy I'm playing it's best you know about my previous games, of which there were two glaring problems, both completely opposite to one another. My first ever ORG I was voted out because I didn't understand the amount of effort you had to put into talking to everyone and therefore I was lacking socially compared to a lot of my other tribemates. My second ORG, I went all out. I had amazing social connections with everyone but people soon caught onto the fact that I had good social connections with everyone and I was voted out for being too much of a threat. This time around I hope to balance the two, playing a very balanced social game in which I have good social connections with everyone to the point where they consider me an ally but not to the point where they would die for me. Playing this type of social game will benefit me in two ways. One, it will allow me to blindside anybody I want while still maintaining their respect at Final Tribal Council because our tight but not close bond will allow them to recognize that their blindside wasn't a harsh betrayal but a good social play. Two, it will make people more inclined to include me in their blindside plans because of the fact that they already feel close to me and know that being in on their plan will not be another point on my already long resume because they will think that I am playing, at best, a mediocre game. To answer your other question, with this ORG I hope to accomplish an epic idol play. It has been my dream to idol out somebody, completely ruining their plan and sending them home with a minority amount of votes like a boss.

Ava 07/21/2023 10:12 PM
Day 1 Summary Now that the first day of Snackvivor is coming to a close I think it's fit I make a summary of all that happened today. This ORG's marooning was a lot different than the other two that I've experienced. I connected with a lot of people without saying much whereas in my last ORG I talked a ton and didn't connect with people as much as I wanted to. There were a couple people who I vibed with a lot, and others where I felt a little bit of a connection, and one person who didn't show up. Trust Rankings 1. Cake
  • Cake is the person I have talked the most with, and the person I have connected the most with. I love his energy, how he always has a quippy response ready to your message, and in general he is always matching my vibe. We have not yet talked strategy, but I hope to tomorrow. For now, he sits on top of these trust rankings like the king he is.
