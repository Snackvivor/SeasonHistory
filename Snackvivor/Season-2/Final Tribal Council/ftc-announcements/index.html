Hello @Castaways and welcome to Final Tribal Council! Our finalists have fought their way through endless battles for a coveted seat at FTC next to their fellow players in Sushitopia, and now the culminating test shall commence… We hope that you are tremendously happy with yourselves, you have helped make this second season of Snackvivor so bright. We’re just as proud of you as you should be of yourselves Please welcome our @Jury - Fones, Dicy, Aura, Slaytan, Etay, Treb and PBJ voted out at F4 tribal council. The power now shifts into their hands. THIS MESSAGE CONTAINS VERY IMPORTANT INFORMATION Please read this top to bottom! It should have all the pertinent details about the next few days. If you have questions, let us know!! Our Finalists will have until 7:30pm EST on Saturday, May 20th to complete their speeches and send them in via their submissions channels. That is 48 hours. We will then post them in this channel, pinning them for easy access, and our jury PREPERATION period will begin. The jury will have 24 hours to read through the speeches and prepare some questions to approach our finalists with. Jurors are NOT allowed to speak about the speeches to any of their fellow players, Ponderosa will be closed. Questioning period begins on Sunday, May 21st at 7:30pm EST. Each Juror will have their own questioning channel that the Finalists will be able to respond in. This will last until 7:30pm EST on Monday, May 22nd. We will also open a FTC general chat during jury for you all to speak in during preparation/questioning, please keep it casual - Game talk is ONLY allowed in the questioning channels. FINALISTS
Your speech may be as long as you want it to be, as long as you can get it to us in a format we can share to the jury and to the public. But we will also be asking you to submit a shorter version of your speech, a TLDR if you will, that fits into the size of a discord message (2000 characters maximum) to make them accessible for those who may not be able to read longer speeches. Your 1-on-1 chats will be closed when the questioning period starts, you may talk in the FTC general chat all you'd like. We have complied a few examples of our hosting team's past FTC speeches for you to view if you'd like inspiration, guidelines, or just to be nosy! Let us know if you'd like to see them, we're more than happy to hand 'em over!!
JURORS
First and foremost, before anything else, we will ask for a kind and respectful questioning period. All of these players worked just as hard as you did - Putting their seaweed, sweat and tears into making it to where they are today! Ponderosa will be closed when the questioning period starts, from then on you may talk in the FTC general chat. You may submit your vote any time over the course of questioning, but it will be due at 7:00pm EST on Monday, May 22nd. ON THE DOT. If it is a second past the hour mark, we will not be accepting it.
Reunion will be held on Monday, May 22nd at 7:30pm EST after all the votes have been collected. We will then read them out, and crown the second winner of Snackvivor - The Executive Chef of Sushitopia! Good luck, Sushomies - We’re just getting started (edited)

TLDR: Dear Jury, The majority of my confessionals have been written in this letter format, so I only figured it appropriate to write one final one: to you. So, what's my final pitch to you? Why me? Because I've Outwitted, Outplayed, and Outlasted better than most realize. I told you all everything when you asked (and sometimes when you didn't), which made everyone trust me more...so they never saw me as a threat until it was too late. Outplay: This is the one I think flies under the radar for me. However, I'm sitting here with the most individual immunity wins out of the three of us. Additionally, remember that I had no idea what I was doing when this started. Yet, I managed to contribute significantly to tribe challenges, as well as perform well individually. Outlast: Everyone at FTC can argue 'oh I outlasted because I'm here.' But let me lay this out real clear: I have been to twelve tribal councils this season and survived every single one. Let's not forget--this is my first ORG and I have outlasted some of the most seasoned veterans we had this season. I've cried more times than I can count this game (just ask Production) and yet I am still here. I did not give up, even when I wanted to. Regardless of how your vote goes, I also want to thank you all. Thank you for making this first ORG experience so memorable and exciting. I love you all and I look forward to your questions and comments. Love, Mana (edited)
