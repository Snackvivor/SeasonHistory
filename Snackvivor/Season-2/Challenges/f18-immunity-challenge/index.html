F18: Coup-nament
19:32
If you haven't heard of Coup before, don't worry - It's quite simple!
19:32
Each player starts with two random cards (a.k.a "Influences") from a set of 15. There are five possible influence roles, with three of each existing in the deck
19:32
On your turn, you may choose an action to play. The action you choose may or may not correspond to the influences that you possess. For the action that you choose, other players may potentially block or challenge it.
19:32
"Challenge: When a player declares an action they are declaring to the rest of the players that they have a certain influence, and any other player can challenge it. When a player is challenged, the challenged player must reveal the correct influence associated with their action. If they reveal the correct influence, the challenger player will lose an influence. However, if they fail to reveal the correct influence the challenged player will lose their incorrectly revealed influence."
19:32
"Block: When the any of the actions ""Foreign Aid"", ""Steal"", and ""Assasinate"" are used, they can be blocked. Once again, any player can claim to have the correct influence to block. However, blocks can also be challenged by any player. If a block fails, the original action will take place."
19:33
If a player loses all their influences, they are out of the game. The last player standing wins!
19:33
As for what the influences are, here is a list of them and what they can do:
19:33
"Captain: STEAL: Steal 2 coins from a target. Blockable by Captain or Ambassador. Can block STEAL"
19:33
"Assassin: ASSASSINATE: Pay 3 coins to choose a target to assassinate (target loses an influence). Blockable by Contessa."
19:33
"Duke: TAX: Collect 3 coins from the treasury. Not blockable. Can block Foreign Aid."
19:34
"Ambassador: EXCHANGE: Draw 2 influences into your hand and pick any 2 influences to put back. Not blockable. Can block STEAL"
19:34
"Contessa: BLOCK ASSASSINATION: Can block assassinations. Not blockable."
19:34
"Regardless of what Influence you have, you can take any of these actions: INCOME: Collect 1 coins from the treasury. FOREIGN AID: Collect 2 coins from the treasury. Blockable by Duke. COUP: Pay 7 coins and choose a target to lose an influence. If a player starts their turn with 10 or more coins, they must Coup. Not Blockable."
19:34
With all those rules in mind, let me get into how the challenge will work!
19:34
There will be four games set-up where one member from each tribe will compete against members of the other tribe for a spot in the finals!
19:35
The winner of each game will be kept anonymous and will use anonymous nicknames for the matches, so you won't know who or which tribes are making it to the finals!
19:35
One more little caveat, each match you will be using anonymous nicknames so you won't know who your opponents are!
19:35
But Fish! Doesn't that mean it's possible for not every tribe to make it into the finals?
19:35
Why yes, yes it does! But we have accounted for that!
19:35
Here is how the finals and winning immunity works:
19:35
If all four tribes are in the finals, then final 2 surviving tribes win immunity
19:36
If only three tribes are in the finals, then final 2 surviving tribes win immunity
19:36
If it's only two tribes, then both win immunity but have the match anyway for suspense
19:36
In the finals, if its one tribe by a miracle, then the other three will have a tie-breaker instead of a finals match
19:36
In the case where only one tribe gets to the finals, the three losing tribes will each send two reps for an anonymous final match with 6 players, last surviving player wins 2nd immunity for their tribe!
19:36
With all that being said, we have already made your pairings randomly and will be sending you a when2meet link shortly so we can schedule your games! The earlier the better so that we can ensure we have time for the finals!
