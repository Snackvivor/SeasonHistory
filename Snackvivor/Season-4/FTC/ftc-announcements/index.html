Hello Snackers, and welcome to Final Tribal Council!

@Pot Pie You three have done what fifteen others could not, and now you get to reap the pies of your labour! We hope that you are tremendously proud of yourselves, you have helped make this season of Snackvivor so very special - and we can't wait to see what will happen next. Please welcome our @Jury - Sierra, Dime, Byron, Kyle, Maxor, Zach, Allen, and Bella, voted out at F4 tribal council. The power now shifts into their hands. Our Finalists will have until 7:30pm EST on SUNDAY, NOVEMBER 12th to complete their speeches and send them in via their submissions channels. We will then post them in this channel, pinning them for easy access, and our questioning period will begin. Each Juror will have their own questioning channel that the Finalists will be able to respond in. This will last until 7:30pm EST on MONDAY, NOVEMBER 13th. We will also open a FTC general chat for you all to speak in, please keep it casual - Game talk is only allowed in the questioning channels.

FINALISTS

Your speech may be as long as you want it to be, as long as you can get it to us in a format we can share to the jury and to the public. We will also be asking you to submit a shorter version of your speech, a TLDR if you will, that fits into the size of a discord message (2000 characters maximum) to make them accessible for those who may not be able to read longer speeches. Your 1-on-1 chats will be closed when questioning period starts, you may talk in the FTC general chat all you'd like.

JURORS

First and foremost, before anything else, we will ask for a kind and respectful questioning period. All of these players worked just as hard as you did - Putting their pie, sweat and tears into making it to where they are today! There is a ZERO tolerance policy for any kind of actual arguing, personal attacks, or anything of the sort. Ponderosa will be closed when questioning period starts, from then on you may talk in the FTC general chat. You may submit your vote any time over the course of questioning, but it will be due at 7:30pm EST on MONDAY, NOVEMBER 13TH Reunion will be held on MONDAY, NOVEMBER 13TH at 8:00pm EST after all the votes are collected. We will then read them out, and crown our FOURTH winner of Snackvivor - The Executive Chef of Piesylvania! Good luck, Pieaways - You've still got a lot of work ahead of ya! (edited)

I connected the most with Zach on the first swap and we quickly bonded - with Zach entrusting me with information regarding the advantages within the server - Zach himself had two advantages and trusted me with that information and had also offered to use one on me to ensure we hit the next swap together. I refused and took my chances - keen to build new social relationships. On the next swap, I managed to make MORE connections with Mia and Kyle - a very powerful duo.
